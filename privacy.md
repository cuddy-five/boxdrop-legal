# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** [DATE]

This Privacy Policy describes how the Boxdrop customer feedback automation application ("the App") collects, uses, and protects information.

## Information We Collect
The App processes limited customer information for the purpose of collecting post-purchase feedback. This may include:
- Customer name
- Phone number
- Purchase confirmation details
- Customer feedback rating (1â€“10)
- Optional feedback message

The App receives purchase-related data from QuickBooks Online and sends SMS messages using Twilio.

## How We Use Information
Information is used solely to:
- Request a customer experience rating via SMS
- Route positive feedback to review requests
- Route negative feedback to store owner follow-up
- Maintain an internal audit trail of messages and outcomes

## SMS Communications
Customers receive a single SMS message requesting a rating.  
Customers may opt out at any time by replying **STOP**.

## Data Storage and Retention
Data is stored securely and retained only as long as necessary to support customer feedback workflows and business records.

## Data Sharing
Customer information is **not sold** or shared with third parties, except:
- Twilio (for SMS delivery)
- Service providers required to operate the App

## Your Choices
Customers may opt out of SMS communication at any time by replying **STOP**.

## Contact
For questions about this policy, contact the business owner directly.
